Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Steam

Steam.com

The clear emphasis on blasting the users face with the newest and biggest released product to create hype for a large audience is a good example of Visual Hierarchy.

Fitt's Law

LDS.org

churchofjesuschrist.org

The Gospel Library section of churchofjesuschrist.org is a very user friendly experience. Having so many pages to go down a rabbit hole with, its designers were very careful to make ease of access a priority.

White Space

GOG.com

gog.com

I really love GOG's use of whitespace. The site is primarily a store, but the way they emphasize the little text they do have with whitespace is very visually appealing.
